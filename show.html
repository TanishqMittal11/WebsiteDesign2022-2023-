Introduction

Alex, Victoria, Ben, Anthony, and Melody have been friends since childhood and love to explore and hang out together. They each have unique personalities that add to their excellent friendships. Victoria is charismatic and curious while Alex is adventurous. Ben finds joy in the little things but can be a bit noisy. Anthony is a team player and always ready to have a good time with his friends and family. Lastly, Melody is enthusiastic and inquisitive. Join this fun-loving crew on their adventures and check out their show, "The 5z Crew"!
